About Our Company

ABOUT

Established in 2014, PinasPrint is a Philippine-based printing business offering both online and physical services. We focus on fast turnaround, affordable bulk pricing, and dependable quality for practical branding needs.

Our core products include customized lanyards, button pins, keychains, shirts, and promotional materials. Every order is handled with the same goal: to help customers present themselves clearly and professionally.
